# Higgsfield Voiceover — paste-ready script

How to use: Higgsfield → **Audio → Voiceover** → paste text → pick an AI audio
model + voice preset (or your cloned voice) → **Generate**.

**Voice direction:** warm, confident, conversational — a friendly member inviting
you in, not a hard sell. Natural pace, a small lift on the question and the CTA.
Good preset feel: a warm mid-range male or female narrator.

---

## Option A — Full narration (~27s)

Paste the whole block as one generation (keeps pacing natural), or generate
line-by-line if you want to time each to a shot.

```
Have you ever imagined a climbing wall just seconds away, instead of an hour?
It's possible — if we join forces.
Right here at Network School. Steps from where we live and work.
Put twenty dollars behind it. A deposit, not a donation — pull it back anytime.
Enough of us, and it happens.
Add your contribution. Scan the code.
```

## After you generate
- Download the Higgsfield audio and drop it over your video edit.
- Subtitles: `voiceover.srt` matches Option A. If you use Option B or the Higgsfield
  pacing differs, tell me and I'll regenerate the SRT to match.
- Prefer a lip-synced delivery? Use **Higgsfield Speak** (talking-head avatar) or
  the **WAN 2.5** model (text-to-video with native audio) instead of a separate VO.
