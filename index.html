We, the undersigned

We want a climbing gym at Network School.

We all love to climb. But Johor Bahru is too far for it to be part of our daily flow.
We, the undersigned, want a climbing gym at Network School — one Lemur board, in a small room, steps from where we live and work.

The whole idea is small.

  • One adjustable Lemur climbing board — that's the gym.
  • A small, bright room: glass, plants, Forest City light.
  • Standing desks along the back wall to work between sessions.
  • A couch, a couple of beanbags, a stool or two. Nothing more.

One board does the work of a whole gym.

The Lemur adjustable Kilter board: thousands of app-tracked LED problems, any angle from slab to steep. Beginner to world-cup on the same wall — and a reason to come back every day.
