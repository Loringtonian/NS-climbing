How we keep it safe & legitimate

The operating model

A climbing gym is a real business with a real safety model — the same one every commercial gym in the world runs on. Here’s how the risk is handled so no host, landlord, or community is ever on the hook.

01

Separate operating companyOwned and run as its own LLC. Liability sits with the business, not with any partner, venue, or community it lives near.

02

Commercial insuranceFull general-liability and participant coverage, the standard policy commercial climbing gyms carry — bound before doors open.

03

Industry-standard waiverEvery climber signs the same assumption-of-risk waiver used across the industry. Digital, on file, required to climb.

04

Bouldering-forward, no lead fallsLow walls over deep crash pads and auto-belays — the lower-injury end of the sport — with certified staff and a managed setting program.
